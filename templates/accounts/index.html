Tested Over Time – More Than a Brand, It's a Testament

Tested Over Time was born from brokenness, built through faith, and refined by the hands of Jesus.

It's not about perfection – it's about perseverance. It's for the ones who've been through the dark, wrestled with doubt, and still chose to stand back up and follow the light.

Every design, every message, every stitch carries a story – not of who we were, but of who God is making us to be.

This brand exists to remind the world that your scars don't disqualify you; they prove that grace is real. That the same hands that broke you can rebuild you stronger, softer, and more aligned with your purpose.

Tested Over Time is a movement for the redeemed – the ones who've been tested, tried, and transformed.

We wear faith out loud.

We walk with purpose.

We remember that every trial was preparation for the mission God placed within us.

Two paths. One soul. One Savior.

That's not a slogan – it's our story.
